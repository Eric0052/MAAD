The Non-functional Requirements are:

1. **NR-001**:

   - The APAF data system is not required to operate in more than one state or mode.

   - Related quality attribute(s): Simplicity, Maintainability

2. **NR-002**:

   - The APAF data system will not impose hazards to personnel, property, or the environment.

   - Related quality attribute(s): Safety, Reliability

3. **NR-003**:

   - The APAF Operations Procedures Document shall provide installation and operations procedures in enough detail to eliminate the need for training-related requirements.

   - Related quality attribute(s): Usability, Efficiency
